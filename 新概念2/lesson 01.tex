\section{Lesson 1 A private conversation}

\begin{paracol}{2}
    

Last week I went to the theatre.

\switchcolumn

上星期我去看戏。

\switchcolumn*

I had a very good seat. 

\switchcolumn

我的座位很好，

\switchcolumn*


The play was very interesting. 

\switchcolumn

戏很有意思，

\switchcolumn*

I did not enjoy it. 

\switchcolumn

但我却无法欣赏。

\switchcolumn*

A young man and a young woman were sitting behind me. 

\switchcolumn


一青年男子与一青年女子坐在我的身后，

\switchcolumn*

They were talking loudly. 

\switchcolumn

大声地说着话。

\switchcolumn*


I got very angry. 

\switchcolumn

我非常生气，

\switchcolumn*

I could not hear the actors. 

\switchcolumn

因为我听不见演员在说什么。

\switchcolumn*

I turned round. 

\switchcolumn

我回过头，

\switchcolumn*

I looked at the man and the woman angrily. 

\switchcolumn

怒视着那一男一女，

\switchcolumn*

They did not pay any attention. 

\switchcolumn

他们却毫不理会。

\switchcolumn*

In the end, I could not bear it. 

\switchcolumn

最后，我忍不住了，

\switchcolumn*

I turned round again. 

\switchcolumn

又一次回过头去，


\switchcolumn*

‘I can't hear a word!' I said angrily.

\switchcolumn

生气地说:“我一个字也听不见了!”

\switchcolumn*

‘It’s none of your business,’ the young man said rudely. 

\switchcolumn


“不关你的事，”那男的毫不客气地说，


\switchcolumn*

‘This is a private conversation!’

\switchcolumn

“这是私人间的谈话!”

\switchcolumn*

\end{paracol}
